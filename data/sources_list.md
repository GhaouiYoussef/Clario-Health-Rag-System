# Suggested Data Sources for Healthcare RAG

Please download the following PDFs and place them in `data/documents/`:

1. **CDC - Influenza (Flu) Guide**
   - URL: https://www.cdc.gov/flu/pdf/freeresources/family/flu-guide-for-parents-2018.pdf
   - Topic: Flu symptoms, prevention, and treatment.

2. **WHO - Physical Activity Strategy**
   - URL: https://iris.who.int/bitstream/handle/10665/272722/9789241514187-eng.pdf
   - Topic: Exercise guidelines and benefits.

3. **NIH/NIDDK - Diabetes Diet, Eating, & Physical Activity**
   - URL: https://www.niddk.nih.gov/-/media/Files/Diabetes/More_than_50_Ways_508.pdf
   - Topic: Diabetes prevention and management.

4. **American Red Cross - First Aid/CPR/AED Participant's Manual**
   - URL: https://www.redcross.org/content/dam/redcross/training-services/no-index/First%20Aid-CPR-AED-Participant-s-Manual.pdf
   - Topic: First aid for burns, cuts, etc.
   - *Note: Large file, good for testing chunking/retrieval speed.*

5. **CDC - Heads Up: Concussion in Youth Sports**
   - URL: https://www.cdc.gov/headsup/pdfs/custom/headsupconcussion_fact_sheet_coaches.pdf
   - Topic: Head injury symptoms and protocol.

6. **WHO - Healthy Diet**
   - URL: https://iris.who.int/bitstream/handle/10665/325828/EMROPUB_2019_en_23536.pdf
   - Topic: Nutrition basics, sugar/salt intake.

7. **CDC - Stop Smoking**
   - URL: https://www.cdc.gov/tobacco/data_statistics/fact_sheets/fast_facts/index.htm (Print to PDF)
   
8. **AHA - Understanding Blood Pressure Readings**
   - URL: https://www.heart.org/-/media/files/health-topics/high-blood-pressure/hbp-rainbow-chart-english-pdf-ucm_475165.pdf
   - Topic: Hypertension definitions.
